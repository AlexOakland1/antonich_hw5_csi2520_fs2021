This video is about Youtuber Zack Freedman buying the parts, preparing the case, and building a new PC after his old one was fried by a broken water cooling pipe. He chooses a large, open case to make sure he can properly maintain the computer to prevent another accident. Then he chooses his RGB strip lights and assembles the build, making sure he gets it right. I found the video to be entertaining, as the production value was good and seeing the process of building the computer was interesting. I also want to build a new computer when the component shortages end, so this video gives me plenty of ideas for what I could do with it.
